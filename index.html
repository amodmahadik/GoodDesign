    Dieter Rams:

    10 Principles for
    Good Design
  • 1
    1

    Good Design is Innovative.

    A product is not only setting the industry-standard for design, but for the most modern form of technology.
  • 2
    2

    Good Design Makes a Product Useful.

    A product not only has aesthetic, but is also functional for the user without distractions.
  • 3
    3

    Good Design is Aesthetic.

    A product’s aesthetic provides the need for the user, but only when necessary.

  • 4
    4

    Good Design Makes a Product Understandable.

    A product’s design speaks to the user, explaining its purppse.

  • 5
    5

    Good Design is Unobtrusive.

    While it fulfills its design needs, a product is not a piece of art, but rather a tool for everyday use.

  • 6
    6

    Good Design is Honest.

    A product does not hide behind its design, but shows its true purpose in conjunction with the design.

  • 7
    7

    Good Design is Long-Lasting.

    A product is timeless.

  • 8
    8

    Good Design is Thorough Down to the Last Detail.

    It is designed with every detail in mind to serve its full purpose.
  • 9
    9

    Good Design is Environmentally Friendly.

    It is designed and developed with conservation in mind and longlasting use.
